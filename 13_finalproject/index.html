I am very happy with how the mechanism works and it is a substantial improvement from the past version. This will enable even easier use of the switches since the base has more tension.</h5> <img width="640" height="640" src="clamp.jpg"> <img width="640" height="640" src="clamp2.jpg"> <h4>3.
